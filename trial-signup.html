Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your free trial

How long is the free trial?

Your free trial lasts for 14 days from the moment you sign up. You'll have full access to all features during this period.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No, you don't need to provide any payment information to start your free trial. We'll only ask for payment details if you decide to continue after the trial ends.

What happens when my trial ends?

At the end of your 14-day trial, you can choose to subscribe to one of our plans to continue using Chatterly. If you don't subscribe, your account will be paused, but your data will be saved for 30 days.

Can I change plans during my trial?

Yes, you can switch between plans at any time during your trial to find the one that best fits your needs.

Is there a limit to how many conversations I can have?

During your trial, you'll have access to the conversation limits of the plan you selected. You can view these limits on our pricing page.

Do you offer support during the trial?

Absolutely! You'll have access to our customer support team throughout your trial period. We're here to help you get the most out of Chatterly.
