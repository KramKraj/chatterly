Getting Started

How do I create my first chatbot?

Creating your first chatbot with Chatterly is easy! After signing up, simply:

  1. Select a template for your industry or use case
  2. Customize the chatbot's appearance to match your brand
  3. Edit the conversation flows to suit your needs
  4. Add the chatbot to your website with our simple code snippet

Our Getting Started Guide provides detailed instructions with screenshots.

Do I need technical skills to use Chatterly?

No technical skills required! Chatterly is designed to be user-friendly for everyone. Our intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to create, customize, and deploy chatbots without any coding knowledge.

Adding the chatbot to your website is as simple as copying and pasting a small code snippet, which we provide with clear instructions.

How quickly can I get started?

You can have your first chatbot up and running in as little as 15 minutes! Our templates are designed to be quickly customized and deployed, so you can start engaging with your website visitors right away.

For more complex implementations or custom integrations, our support team is available to help you get set up quickly.

Features & Functionality

Can I integrate Chatterly with my existing tools?

Yes! Chatterly integrates with popular CRM systems, email marketing platforms, and other business tools, including:

  • HubSpot
  • Salesforce
  • Mailchimp
  • Zapier (connecting to 3,000+ apps)
  • Google Analytics
  • And many more

Check our integrations page for a complete list of supported platforms.

Can I customize the appearance of my chatbot?

Absolutely! You can fully customize the appearance of your chatbot to match your brand, including:

  • Colors and fonts
  • Chat bubble shape and position
  • Avatar or logo
  • Welcome message and button text
  • Chat window size and style

Our visual editor makes it easy to see your changes in real-time.

Can I have both automated responses and live chat?

Yes! Chatterly offers a hybrid approach that combines automated chatbot responses with live chat capabilities. Your chatbot can handle common questions and tasks automatically, then seamlessly hand off to a human agent when needed.

This hybrid approach gives you the best of both worlds: 24/7 automated support with the personal touch of human conversation when it matters most.
